## Il Problema (come lo racconti)

Quando un'azienda passa da 10 a 20 a 40 persone, non raddoppia solo l'organico — raddoppia la complessità.

A 10, la velocità compensa il disordine. A 20, le decisioni iniziano a rallentare. A 40, passi l'80% del tempo a spegnere fuochi e ti chiedi perché la crescita si è fermata.

The real issue isn't talent. It's that the company grew faster than its structure. Nobody fully owns the outcome. Every decision still runs through the founder. And value is leaking in the handoffs between teams that don't talk to each other.

> **Nota:** Questi sono sintomi, non accuse. Il founder li legge e pensa "sì, è la mia azienda." Nessuno di questi dice "sei tu il problema."

## Chi lavora con me

Tech and marketplace companies, post-seed or Series A, **€1–15M revenue**. Typically 15–80 people. Companies where the growth is real but the structure hasn't kept up.

---

## The 3S Method™

**1. Structure**
Where decisions stall, who actually owns what, where value leaks in the handoffs between teams.

**2. Systems**
New governance, clear ownership, and processes designed for the stage you're actually at — not the one you've outgrown.

**3. Scale**
Real accountability. A machine that runs without the founder in the room.

---

## Tre porte d'ingresso

**Fractional COO**
Leadership operativa continuativa. Lavoro con il tuo team su base regolare — costruisco struttura, guido l'esecuzione, rimuovo i blocchi. Per aziende con product-market fit, pronte a scalare.

**Consultant COO**
Progetti specifici con scope chiaro: ridisegno organizzativo, ristrutturazione team, integrazione M&A. Entro, risolvo, e te lo riconsegno.

**Advisory**
Per founder che hanno bisogno di un sparring partner. Check-in regolari per sfidare il tuo ragionamento, rivedere la struttura, e tenerti onesto sull'esecuzione.

> **Nota:** Niente prezzi qui. Niente durate. Il founder sceglie la porta, poi se ne parla.

## What makes me different (pitch + one-pager, tono elegante)

1. **I read the P&L and redesign the org.** Most fractional COOs come from pure ops. I have real P&L ownership and Big Four financial training. When I say "I find where margin leaks," it's not a metaphor.

2. **I make the calls nobody inside wants to make.** 20+ performance transitions managed. Direct pushback on founders when needed. I'm not there to build consensus — I'm there to build a company that works.

3. **I'm not part of your politics.** I come in without legacy relationships or internal loyalties. I see what's actually happening, not what people want me to see.

4. **I've seen this pattern before.** Five companies, same story: departments that don't talk to each other, margin lost in the handoffs, founders stuck in the middle of every decision.

5. **Trilingual, EU-based.** Italian, English, Spanish. Barcelona. For European companies operating cross-border, this is a practical advantage.
